Olympic Swimming Pool, Agadir, Morocco (concept) – 2022

This sustainable Olympic‑pool concept optimises space for international competitions and diverse sports activities. The design integrates with the urban environment; its flowing silhouette recalls a wave, combining appearance and functionality while expressing the movement of water. Developed within Zohry and Partners; includes concept design, planning, facades, interiors and modeling (Revit, 3ds Max).

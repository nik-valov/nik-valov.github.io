Youth Palace “Zhastar”, Astana, Kazakhstan (concept) – 2022

The palace is contained within a 65×65×65 m cube where blocks of varying shapes and sizes are connected by bright staircases and elevators. Each block serves a distinct function—children’s center, concert hall, discussion club, media center—using different colours and textures. The rigid crystalline grid juxtaposes dynamic and static, bringing order to multicoloured chaos. Developed with Studio 44 through sketch, project and rendering stages.

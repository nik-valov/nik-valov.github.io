City of Trades and Skills, Marrakesh‑Safi, Morocco – 3rd place (2022)

A university complex that unites various students and reflects the blend of traditional past and innovative future. A pergola inspired by a neural network covers the central square and links buildings with local architectural elements, symbolising Morocco’s classical training system. Developed within the Zohry and Partners agency; project includes conceptual design, planning, facades, interiors and modeling.
